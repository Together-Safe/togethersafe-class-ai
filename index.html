About CLASS AI

CLASS AI is TogetherSafe’s advanced platform for real-time driver and operator monitoring, predictive maintenance, and transport safety analytics. Using multimodal sensor fusion — including heart rate variability, skin response, and vehicle telemetry — CLASS AI proactively identifies cognitive fatigue, distraction, and risk, supporting fleets and logistics operators to prevent accidents and optimize operations.

Key Features

  • Continuous, AI-driven operator inspection
  • Predictive risk and fatigue alerts
  • User-friendly dashboards and compliance reporting
  • Edge-ready and cloud analytics platform
  • Designed for fleets, logistics, connected & autonomous vehicles
